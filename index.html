About Me

I’m Echo. I don’t design for attention, I design for understanding. I believe the best interfaces are like birdsong at dawn: purposeful, fleeting, and easy to miss if you’re not listening. You won’t find noise in my work, only clarity. Every interaction I design is quiet on the surface and deeply considered underneath. I shape space with whitespace. I write code with rhythm. I animate only when it speaks. I don’t scream for clicks. I wait until you arrive and make sure it feels like home.
